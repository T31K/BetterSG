Better.sg is a fast-growing non-profit, volunteer-run organisation that drives techforgood in Singapore. We address societal problems with digital tools. We are an inclusive and multidisciplinary team, and our well-structured modular virtual process allows busy talents from non-technical (dreamers, disruptors, do-ers) and technical (designers, developers, data scientists) backgrounds to collaborate to understand social problems, identify areas where tech can help, and build and launch our tools or help other organisations use technology to do good.
